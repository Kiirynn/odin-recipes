    • Naan Pizza

    Description

    Pizza night has never been easier! 10-minute Naan Pizza that has a golden crispy crust, tons of cheese, and is so yummy and quick. Your family will love this simple recipe that takes no time at all to make!

    ingredients

    • naan
    • mozzarella cheese
    • parmesan cheese
    • pizza sauce
    • fresh chopped basil

    The Steps

    1. use a homemade pizza sauce recipe or your favorite store bought.
    2. Sprinkle shredded or sliced mozzarella and a little parmesan cheese. You can also use your favorite Italian cheese blend
    3. Add even more flavor to your pizza by utilizing your spice cabinet, using fresh herbs, or adding more cheeses! First, let’s start with the cheese. Since mozzarella doesn’t have the most flavor, we love adding freshly grated parmesan over top before baking! It adds a salty, nutty flavor and melts beautifully.
    4. When the pizza is finished, we hit the spice cabinet or use up our fresh herbs on hand. We love adding fresh chopped basil or oregano, or using dried oregano for an herb flavor. Another must is red pepper flakes for a little spicy kick! These ingredients are so simple yet really pack on the flavor
    5. Who doesn’t love pizza toppings?! And since these pizza’s are so small, you can easily personalize them to make things fun and even more delicious. Our favorite toppings are: roasted vegetables, sliced olives, pepperoni, cooked meatballs, and cooked Italian sausage.
