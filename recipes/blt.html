    Description

    AVOCADO BLT RECIPE This avocado BLT recipe creates a wonderful lunch sandwich. Everyone loves BLTsâ€¦ but when you add avocado slices & grill the sandwich bread in butter, it is heavenly!!! Of course, BLTs are even better when they are made with homemade bacon bread. Double bacon deliciousness!

    ingredients

    • 1/2 small ripe avocado, peeled
    • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
    • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
    • 2 tablespoons crumbled Gorgonzola cheese
    • 4 slices Italian bread, toasted
    • 1-1/2 cups fresh baby spinach
    • 4 tomato slices
    • 4 thick-sliced applewood smoked bacon strips, cooked

    The Steps

    1. Mash avocado with a fork; stir in mayonnaise and lemon juice. Gently stir in cheese.
    2. Spread over toasts. Top 2 of the slices with spinach, tomato, bacon and remaining slices of toast.
